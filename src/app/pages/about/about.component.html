The SHOP

DOCTOR VINYL, situated in the heart of Brussels, stands as one of the pioneering vinyl shops in Belgium, specialized in electronic music. A beacon for electronic music enthusiasts, our small and cozy shop is a haven for those seeking the authentic warm analog sounds. At Doctor Vinyl, we pride ourselves on curating a collection of used and occasionaly new vinyl that is meticulously organized and carefully selected. As explorers of fresh grooves, we offer a diverse array of subcategories within electronic music, including House, Techno, D&B, Nu Beat, Industrial, experimental, and everything in between. Our shop is not just a place to buy vinyl; it's a space where music lovers come together to share their passion. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff are here to guide you and ensuring that each visit is a unique and enriching experience. As one of the first establishments of its kind in Belgium, Doctor Vinyl has become a cornerstone in the cultural tapestry of Brussels. Step into our world, where the crackle of vinyl and the pulse of electronic beats merge to create an unforgettable a shopping experience.
